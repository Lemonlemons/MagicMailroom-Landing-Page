Let your residents know when they get a delivery right away

Magic Mailroom is your simple and powerful delivery notification tool

Here is how it works: Add your residents to the magic mailroom by entering their email addresses and apartment numbers. Whenever a package is received by the office, simply enter the room number in our software and press submit. The resident then receives a customizable email saying that they have a package waiting for them in the office~!

Step 1. Add Residents to your Mailroom

Enter residents email addresses and their room numbers. This is how we'll know where to send the notification.

Step 2. Receive a Package and send a Delivery Notification

Whenever you receive a delivery; simply enter the apartment number of the address into Magic Mailroom.

Step 3. Resident receives a Notification Email

The intended resident receives a customizable email informing them that a delivery is waiting for them!
